Sebastien Galina

Web Designer, Backend Developer, and Digital Art Director since 2009

I work with HTML5, CSS3, Django, Python, Apache, Linux, and JQuery to build small sites and big sites. I have experience with database design, scalable cloud systems like Amazon Web Services, and frontend design. I've posted my website's style guide online, and the rest of the codebase as well.

In my spare time, I work on film and photography projects for big brands such as The City of Surrey, Richmond, Universal Music, and Coast Capital Savings.

Education

I graduated from Simon Fraser University with a BSc in Computing Science, but in addition to having learned the ins and outs of software development, artificial intelligence and TCP/IP networking hardware, I studied graphic design and web design principles in SIAT, the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at SFU.

2015

Boldly Creative Agency

I started a creative agency with two business partners; we've shot commercials and projects for A&W, Deloitte, Playland and Coast Capital. We go beyond the traditional production company by offering creative campaigns for small to medium sized businesses, which includes branding, photography, commercials and web.

2015

Exposure Contest

I built a website to host a video editing contest. We garnered over 40k views, had over 280 participants sign up from all over the world. After shooting a music video in Vancouver, we uploaded the HD footage online and gave it away for free. Those who chose to enter our contest won the chance to direct and edit a short film with my team!

2013

I worked as a software developer (co-op) in the VFX hub of Vancouver, BC. I built desktop programs with Python and PyQT to assist the artist floor and company infrastructure. During my time there, I learned a lot about colour theory, enterprise level software distribution, and contributed to an ever-evolving set of production software tools and databases.

2012
